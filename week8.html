WOT ARE WE DOING IN WEEK 8

This week, we're moving from simply making things light up to controlling them interactively!

From Electrical to Computing?

Last week, we learned how to power an LED and make it light up.

This week, we're learning how to use resistors and buttons to manually control those circuits.

It's our first step into Physical Computing: not just making things move, but teaching them to respond. This is where **programming meets hardware**.
